Quick Answers

How do I place an order?

Fill out our online order form and we'll confirm on WhatsApp within minutes.

How is the delivery fee calculated?

Delivery fee is based on your distance from us in Warri and will be communicated and agreed on WhatsApp before payment.

How long does it take?

All orders are ready within 48 hours of collection. We'll notify you on WhatsApp when your clothes are ready.

Can I walk in and drop off?

Yes! Walk-ins are welcome Monday–Friday (8:30AM–6PM) and Saturday (8:30AM–4PM). No appointment needed.

When do I pay?

Payment is made at collection/delivery after we confirm your order and agree on the final amount (including any delivery fee) on WhatsApp.

Do you handle delicate fabrics?

Yes. We sort by fabric type and colour to ensure every garment is treated with the right care.
